I'm proud to be part of a [Guinness World Records] attempt, running in a non-stop four-week relay around [Princes Park] to help [conquer cancer]. It also happens that [my little brother ran into the record books][tcs-london-marathon-extends-record-as-biggest-marathon-ever] this weekend.

[![Steve and me running at the Cardiff Half Marathon in 2019][conquer-cancer-hero]][Please donate if you can]

Every dollar I raise will support [Camp Quality]'s programs for kids facing cancer and fund vital research at [The Florey Institute].

[Please donate if you can]. Your support helps bring hope, laughter and life-changing breakthroughs to families impacted by cancer.

Every donation, big or small, will help keep the baton moving and make a real difference.

If you want to come along to Princes Park and run a lap with me, cheer me on, or just have a laugh at me, I'll be there at the following times:

- 07:00 - 08:00 on Wednesday, 6 May 2026
- 06:00 - 07:00 on Wednesday, 13 May 2026
- 06:00 - 07:00 on Wednesday, 20 May 2026

Thank you ever so much for backing me! I really appreciate it. And the best of luck to everyone taking part in [the World’s Longest Relay to Conquer Cancer][conquer cancer].
