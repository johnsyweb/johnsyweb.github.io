## Speaker bio (long)

Pete Johns is a Melbourne-based software engineering leader and community organiser focused on building, maintaining, and running reliable and resilient systems with happy teams. He builds open-source tools, shares lessons from real projects, and focuses on solutions that people can apply in everyday work.

Outside work, Pete is deeply involved in parkrun as an event-day volunteer and participant, and as a volunteer ambassador supporting other ambassadors and event teams. He brings that same mindset to technology: reduce friction, lower barriers, and design systems that help people thrive.

Pete is also a cardiac arrest survivor. That experience has sharpened his perspective on resilience, prevention, and the value of communities that show up for each other. His talks are practical, candid, and grounded in lived experience.
